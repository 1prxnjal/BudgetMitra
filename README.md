# 💰 BudgetMitra – India Ka Smart Budget Saathi

**BudgetMitra** is a premium, AI-powered financial companion designed to help Indian users track their wealth, manage liabilities, and get real-time financial advice. Powered by **Groq LPU™ technology** and **Llama 3.3**, it offers near-instant financial intelligence with a deep understanding of the Indian economy (UPI, ₹, Indian Tax contexts).

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **🤖 Saathi AI Chatbot**: A high-speed, domain-locked financial assistant that analyzes your spending and gives personalized advice.
- **📊 Interactive Dashboard**: Real-time visualization of your financial health, including available balance, monthly income, and expense breakdowns.
- **🏦 Wealth Portfolio**: Track your Savings, Fixed Deposits (FDs), and Investments (Stocks/Mutual Funds) in one place.
- **🚨 Loan Manager**: Advanced tracking for all your liabilities, including Principal amounts, Lenders, and automated EMI commitment calculation.
- **📱 Premium Glassmorphism UI**: A state-of-the-art interface with smooth transitions, dark-mode elements, and a high-fidelity "Pro" feel.
- **🚀 Ultra-Low Latency**: Responses in under 1 second thanks to Groq's LPU™ inference engine.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React.js (Vite), Lucide-React, ReactMarkdown, Axios
- **Backend**: FastAPI & Flask (Python)
- **AI Engine**: Llama 3.3 70B (via Groq SDK)
- **Database**: Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- **Authentication**: Supabase Auth (Email & Google OAuth)
- **Deployment**: Vercel / Render
